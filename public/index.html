Frequently Asked Questions

How do I register for the student portal?

To register, click on the "Register" button at the top of the page. You'll need your student ID, official email, and personal information to complete the registration process.

When does online enrollment start?

Online enrollment typically begins two weeks before the start of each semester. Specific dates are announced through the college's official communication channels.

How can I pay my tuition fees online?

You can pay your tuition fees through the payment section in the student portal. We accept various payment methods including credit/debit cards, bank transfers, and digital wallets.

What should I do if I forgot my password?

Click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. You'll receive a password reset link to your registered email address.
